INK ASYLUM


Located in South Kirkby, Pontefract Ink Asylum is a newly opened studio found in the hair and beauty salon 'CH House Of Beauty'. Although his studio is still in its early days, Christopher Bowen has been a remote artist dedicating his time and effort into ensuring every client leaves satisfied. His tattoos vary from original designs he crafts or already made designs ready for needle-to-skin.

About Us

Havign found his muse, Chris has quickly dedicated great time and effort into making sure every client left with the satisfaction he had promised. Chris has delved into creating peices with themes such as manga imagery, grunge work, high-detailed pieces and a little bit of realism with many more styles to come! Chris hopes to broaden his horizons with the different genres and masters each style thrown at him. With his Ipad always at hand, he's constantly creating new ideas for potential tattoos in the future. Chris works closely in charity work and often holds flash sales with a percentage of his earnings goings to organisations dedicated to help those surrounding mental health issues and support networks.
